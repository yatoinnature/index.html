The beginning

I have started fishing since i was 9 years old.

my dad took me to the paid fishing lake and i caught my very first cat fishing.

the feeling delivered to my hand could not be forgotten.

since then, i always asked to dad to go fishing whenever it is possible

However, when i grew up, it stopped somehow.

my first genre of fishing was carp with float fishing using long stick shape to check wherther fishes take bite or not

the second genre is lake lure fishing. the fish called ssogari living in the widely in Korean lakes is main target.

after many years, i began to fish again but this time not with many dad but with friends

this time i learnt that not only fishing is fun but also begin to understand and feel the atomosphere given by the surrounding nature.

for 3 to 4 years, i went to ssogari fishing whenever i have some free time.

One time, a kin asked me to go octopus fishing. i never tried sea fishing before.

the distance from my home to sea is so far, and the cost of sea fishing is so high so i hesitate to go.

however, he persuaded me and i was on the bus to go Yosu.

the time i caught the octopus my fishing life is totaly changed.

from that time, i bought all differnet kinds of sea fishing gears and tries every genre of sea fishing from octopus to red sea bream.

in these days, i tried to learn cuttlefish. let us hope that i could be the master of cuttle fishing
